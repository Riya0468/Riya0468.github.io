Information Technology(I.T.)

Information Technology is a sector that deals with hardware, software, networking etc. In simple words it is basically a system or technology that facilitates the communication or we can also say that anything getting involved in the transmittal of information is known as information technology(I.T.). It is also a technology used to store, manipulate, distribute, or create information. The present modernized generation is the generation of technology. Today, even a simple task is done with the help of technology so, we can say that in the present generation, the information technology is playing a huge role.

News and Media

Safety

In the case of saftey, information technology has provided both security and insecurity to the users. The devices like IOT safety devices keeps our home and belongings safe when we are not physically present there. Cameras have become more advanced to spy or also observe. On the other hand, we are not secure as well because our information or data may get leaked out or may go to the wrong hands which causes a great problem.
